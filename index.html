Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students to speak with confidence, connect through communication, and inspire change through the power of words.

Club's Focus

Build Confidence

Through regular speaking and constructive feedback sessions designed to reduce anxiety and build poise.

Develop Skills

Hone strong communication, active listening, and leadership abilities essential for academic and professional success.

Supportive Community

A safe, encouraging space where members learn from each other, celebrate progress, and grow together.

Campus Engagement

We host events that foster self-expression, spark meaningful dialogue, and amplify student voices across campus.

Semester Timeline

Our club operates on a semester-based schedule with monthly themes and weekly meetings. Each semester begins with an orientation session, followed by skill-building workshops, peer practice sessions, and culminates in our annual showcase event where members present their best speeches to the campus community.

October 01, 2025

Public Speaking Club Kicks Off Fall Semester

Public Speaking Club held its first Fall semester meeting on Wednesday, October 1st, marking an exciting start for students interested in building confidence and communication skills. The club provides opportunities to develop and showcase speaking abilities through debates, presentations, discussions, and social interactions. The meeting began with an icebreaker to help members get to know each other and set a friendly, welcoming tone. It promises to be a fun and engaging semester exploring all that the club has to offer.
